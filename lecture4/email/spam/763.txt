Juniper Group, Inc. Signs a Letter of Intent To Acquire Eyeblast, Inc.

GREAT NECK, N.Y., Dec. 21 /PRNewswire/ -- Juniper Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: JUNI 
<http://finance.yahoo.com/q?s=juni&d=t> - news <http://biz.yahoo.com/n/j/juni.
html>; "Juniper'') announced today that it has entered into a letter of intent 
to acquire Eyeblast, Inc. ("Eyeblast''). Eyeblast, a recently formed company 
has assembled a management team that they believe can implement the business 
plan that will enable them to become a broadband internet company that will 
create various networks of entertainment content. Eyeblast intends to acquire 
the rights, license, and create high quality media for full screen, real time, 
viewing over the web. The transaction is expected to close in early 2000. 
Vlado P. Hreljanovic, chairman and CEO of Juniper Group, commented, "We 
believe that this acquisition brings our entertainment division to the cutting 
edge of entertainment technology for the twenty-first century.''
 
Juniper Group, Inc. operates in the healthcare management area through two 
subsidiaries of Juniper Medical Systems, Inc., which offers medical revenue 
enhancement to hospitals and cost containment to managed care health groups, 
insurance companies, and other medical entities. Juniper Group also operates 
in the entertainment area through its subsidiary, Juniper Entertainment, Inc., 
which distributes feature films to various media, including television and 
cable.
