RADIOLOGICAL SCIENCES UNIT

MR Research Physicist

Grade B11-13 £22,548-£24,217 Pay award pending

Applications are invited for a MR Research Physicist to support a range
of clinically-related research programmes in functional
MRI as applied to neurology and psychiatry. Current and pending projects
include drug induced psychosis and its relationship
to schizophrenia, neuropathology of bipolar disorder, cerebral perfusion
changes following carotid endarterctomy, real time
imaging of cerebral circulation, and the adaption of visual search
following brain injury. In particular the postholder will be
expected to develop MR perfusion imaging methodologies.
The appointment will be made within the Radiological Sciences Unit at
Charing Cross Hospital. The post is available for 24
months from September 1 1999.

Ideally the postholder will
? Have a PhD in MRI
? Have experience of working in hospital and research environments
? Have experience of MRI pulse sequence and image processing software
? Be familiar with Unix and Windows environments
? Possess practical, communication and organisational skills.
