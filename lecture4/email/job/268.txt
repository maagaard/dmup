University College London
Institute of Cognitive Neuroscience and Department of Psychology

Postdoctoral Research Scientist

Applications are invited for a postdoctoral position in the Cognitive
Electrophysiology Group (head: Prof M.D. Rugg) of the Institute of
Cognitive Neuroscience. In collaboration with the Functional Imaging
Laboratory of the Wellcome Department of Cognitive Neurology, the
post-holder will have responsibility for a project using ERPs and
event-related fMRI to study the effects of aging on the neural
correlates of memory encoding and retrieval. Applicants should have
prior experience with ERP or functional neuroimaging methods, and an
interest in the cognitive neuroscience of memory and aging.

The post will run for 3 years in the first instance. Salary will be on
the RA I scale, starting between £18,915 and £23,521 pa (plus £2134 pa
London allowance) depending on age and experience.

Applications (in triplicate) including the names of two referees should
be lodged with Mr John Draper, Department of Psychology, University
College London, Gower Street, London WC1E 6BT (j.draper@ucl.ac.uk), not
later than 11th of February. Further information about the post can be
obtained from Prof. M.D. Rugg, Institute of Cognitive Neuroscience
(email: m.rugg@ucl.ac.uk)
