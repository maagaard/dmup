'Virtual Environments' is the European event for Virtual Reality
research, technology, and applications. Experts from both academia and
industry will gather to discuss the latest trends and ideas. Topics
include immersive VR, distributed VR, VR on the internet, PC-based VR
etc.

The YUFORIC meeting of the IEEE Computer Society will focus on 
virtual environments but will also cover related topics such as
computer graphics, animation, and computer vision. Senior students and
young professionals are invited to present their ideas and get
feedback from experts in their field.
