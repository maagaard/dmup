Dear Colleague:

This is an invitation to a scientific meeting in cyberspace, with close to
1000 presentations in Invited Symposia, Poster Sessions, and the Exhibitors'
Foyer.  Discussions are conducted on the Web from Dec 7-16, 1998 and form an
integral part of the presentations.  Attendance is open and free of charge.

Disciplines covered at the Congress range from basic science to medicine,
education, informatics, and public health.  Keynote Addresses will be given
by Drs Floyd Bloom (Editor of Science), Allan W Cowley Jr (Medical College
of Wisconsin), Henry Friesen (President of Medical Research Council of
Canada) and Sumio Murase (Shinshu University School of Medicine).

Deadline for abstract submission is September 30, 1998; for details, visit
INABIS '98 Congress site at http://www.mcmaster.ca/inabis98/
