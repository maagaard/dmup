SHARC '99
International Conference
November 29, - December 1, 1999
Harfield, Hertfordshire, UK

http://www.sharc99.herts.ac.uk.

SHARC '99 is the first international technical conference for those who
are
involved in designing, developing, researching and discussing innovative
ideas
on applications for DSPs, particularly the Analog Devices SHARC and the 
ADSP-21xx families.

SHARC '99 will feature technical papers and poster presentations in
addition to
a number of 1 or 2 hour training classes, workshops and a technical
exhibition.
The SHARC '99 Technical Review Committee, which is comprised of
distinguished
members of academia and industry, will review all the proposed papers to 
ensure excellence.

Who Should Attend?
This is a unique opportunity for DSP design engineers to meet other
leading
engineers and programmers from around the world. Those who will benefit
from 
this exciting new conference include:

        DSP Software Developers
        Communications & Network Developers
        Instrumentation and Control System Designers
        Consultant & System Analysts
        Defense Electronics Manufacturers
        Educators & Researchers

For more information and registration go to the conference web site
http://www.sharc99.herts.ac.uk
