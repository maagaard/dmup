All abstracts accepted for presentation will be included in the RHFM
Proceedings in hard-copy and in electronic format on diskette, which
will be distributed to you at the conference. All abstracts will
undergo peer review using the following criteria:

  * Quality, originality, timeliness, and clarity of writing &
    presentation

  * Potential significance to theory, application, policy or practice
    in healthcare financial management and related professions

  * Relevance to practice, research, education or public service

  * Completed or nearly completed research, practice demonstration
    projects or case studies

  * Abstracts from a diversity of countries are strongly encouraged

Please carefully adhere to the following instructions:

  * All abstracts, resumes or position postings must be limited to no
    more than one 81/2" ×11" or A4 page, typed in a 10-point or larger
    font

  * Include a concise title that reflects the content of your
    abstract or presentation

  * List the names of all authors after the title, including complete
    institutional affiliation, surface mailing address, telephone,
    fax, and email address of all authors. Asterisk (*) the name of
    the author who will present the paper at the meeting

  * All presenters must be registered participants for the Symposium
    & Workshop. Papers withdrawn or without a participating author
    will not be eligible for inclusion on the program or in the
    Conference Proceedings

  * Style requirements for the Conference Proceedings will be sent
    upon confirmation of your abstract acceptance by March 31, 2000

  * Be sure to indicate your desire to serve in a leadership role as
    a Section Chair or a Track Chair, and specify your topical areas
    of interest
