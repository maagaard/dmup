                                   IPMU'98

                         The Seventh Conference on
                    Information Processing and Management of
                     Uncertainty in Knowledge-Based Systems

                               July 6 - 10, 1998
                                 Paris, France

                    ---------------------------------------------

          The IPMU Conference is organized every two years with the aim of
bringing together scientists working on various methods for the management
of uncertainty in intelligent systems. This conference provides a medium
for the exchange of ideas between theoreticians and practitioners using
different methods to address the important issue of uncertainty.
         This year, the conference is being held in Paris, a city that
provides many attractions for the visitor, as well as being a city with a
rich history in scientific and academic matters. For more information about
Paris, contact the Paris homepage.

 --------------------------------------
