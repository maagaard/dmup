The 1998 Functional Neuroimaging in the Epilepsies Symposium will be at The
Swissotel, Atlanta, Georgia October 30 -Noveember 1. The symposium is
organized by Dr Tom Henry (Atlanta), Prof John Duncan (London) and Prof Sam
Berkovic (Melbourne), under the auspices of The Emory University School of
Medicine.
Further information and registration details can be obtained from:

research techniques and their application to the epilepsies. The program is:
