              Trondheim 26.06 1998

Hello again to all of you !

The organising committee have not received much interest
from students outside Norway  to participate on
the workshop. This may be due to a high registration fee
of NOK 1850,-.

The organising committee have however succeeded to
get financial support from NORFA to cover some of
the expenses. The new registration fee is NOK 600,-.

We hope those of you participating  last year, will
promote this arrangement among your fellow students,
and even consider to join the workshop yourself.

We hope those of you already being willing to participate
will use the time until the new deadline (below) to
inspire your fellow students to join the arrangement.

The new time schedule
       15 May          Second announcement.
NOTE ->17 July         Deadline for submitting abstracts.
       20 July         Acceptance notice to participants (per email).
       24 July         Deadline for registration and payment.
       4-6 September   2nd workshop.
