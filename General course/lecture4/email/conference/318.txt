>Ph.D. students and others engaged in multimedia research coming from a =

>it is held prior to the ECSCW conference
>(http://www.cti.dtu.dk/cscw/ecscw99.html) with which we hope to achieve
>much synergy.
>Purpose and Goal:
>The purpose of the Research school is to bring Ph.D. students from the
>Nordic countries together with internationally well-respected =
researchers
>in their field of interest. The subject is "Interaction and =
Collaboration
>in Virtual Environments", and it focuses on various aspects of =
distributed
>multimedia technologies and applications including collaboration =
support,
>communication, virtual and augmented reality, collaborative learning,
>network organizations, Internet and Intranet applications etc.
