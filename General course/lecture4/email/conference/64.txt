                   CALL FOR PAPERS
Over the past decade or so, neural networks have emerged 
as a research area with active involvement by researchers 
from a number of different disciplines, including cognitive 
science, computer science, engineering, mathematics, 
neurobiology, physics, and statistics. The theme for the 
International Conference on Neural Network and Brain (NN&B'98) 
is to provide a chance for interdisciplinary collaboration and 
exchange of ideas which will lead us to address research issue 
in this area from different perspectives, and to promote its 
application to industries. This conference is intended to bring 
together researchers from different disciplines to review  the 
current status of neural networks and understand higher brain 
functions. Submissions of papers related, but not limited, to 
the topics listed below are invited.
