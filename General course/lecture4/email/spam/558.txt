                Centrack International 

Reaches Goal of Having More Than $1 Billion Worth of Used Heavy Equipment Listed for Sale on Its Web Site


BOCA RATON, Fla., Aug. 30,  /PRNewswire/ -- Centrack International (OTC Bulletin Board: CENK - news), a worldwide Internet resource for the buying  and selling of heavy equipment, announced today that it now has crossed the $1 billion threshold, with $1,045,527,321 worth of heavy equipment listed for sale on its Web site. The announcement was made by John J. Lofquist, President and CEO of Centrack International, Inc.

``We are pleased to have reached this milestone, as it demonstrates our progress in increasing the volume of equipment on the Web  site, as well as marketing our  services and drawing new customers,'' Lofquist said. 

The value of the equipment listed for sale at the Centrack Web site topped $1 billion with the addition of more than $100 million worth of equipment listed by  Western United Industries. Based in North Hollywood, California, Western United Industries specializes in the brokerage, sale and acquisition of power plant  equipment including generators and complete power stations. 

``We chose to list our equipment on Centrack's site because it offers exposure to buyers around the world, and we expect that will help us to make sales,'' said  a representative of Western United Industries. 

Centrack International, Inc. , serves the heavy equipment industry with business-to- business  resources for buying and selling used heavy equipment, supplies and services, and is a public company trading under the stock symbol "CENK."
